Election Day Declared a Special Holiday

May 12, 2025 has been declared a special, non-working holiday to allow Filipinos to cast their votes in the national and local midterm elections. This move is aimed at increasing voter turnout and supporting democratic participation.

About the Commission on Elections

The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) is the constitutional commission responsible for enforcing all laws related to elections in the Philippines. From voter registration to vote counting, COMELEC ensures a fair, honest, and orderly election process.

Ongoing Projects

  • Voter Education Campaigns in rural areas
  • Development of an Online Voter Portal
  • Modernized Vote Counting Machines (VCMs)
  • Inclusive Voting for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs)
