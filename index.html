May 12, 2025 a special, non-working day for midterm elections: Palace

The Commission on Elections personnel, in coordination with the Quezon City Local Government Unit, load election paraphernalia including sealed ballot boxes and vote counting machines onto traditional jeepneys at the Amoranto Sports Complex on Saturday, May 3, 2025. The deployment is part of the early distribution of materials to various polling precincts across the city in preparation for the May 12 National and Local Elections. Security personnel and election officers are also on-site to ensure proper handling and transport of the equipment. Maria Tan, ABS-CBN News

MANILA — Malacañang on Tuesday declared May 12, 2025 a special, non-working holiday for the midterm elections.
